Rums

Carúpano Legendario

The rum displays a deep amber color with yellow highlights and high viscosity. Drink from a balloon glass dry or with a single ice cube to appreciate its color, aroma and flavor.

Santa Teresa 1796

Santa Teresa 1796 is a bold and elegant rum with blends up to 35 years aged in bourbon oak barrels, then further aged through the artisanal Solera method resulting in a dry, smooth and balanced rum.

Roble Extra Añejo

Derived from the ageing of the rum in 220 litre ex-bourbon oak barrels, Ron Roble Extra Añejo is a blend of rums aged between 8 and 12 years.

Veroes Añejo

Venezuelan aged rum, which guarantees the unique color, taste and smell were obtained naturally, as it is prohibited in Venezuela to change these properties through artificial processes.

Cacique 500 Extra Añejo

Dark rum obtained through the mix of differently aged rums, it's particular taste and smell are in no small part the result of having a small part of Cherry.
